LIBRARY DISCIPLINE

GENERAL LIBRARY DISCIPLINE

1) All students must present their library ID card and sign the logbook upon entering the library.

2) Students must wear their uniform.

3) Non-member users may use the library subject for the compliance of non-member requirements mentioned in the library handbook.

4) Bags, envelopes, umbrellas, jacket not use and the like should be left at the baggage counter provided.

5) OBSERVE SILENCE. The library is a place for research and studying; therefore eating, smoking, unnecessary conversations are strictly prohibited.

6) Keep the library clean. Do not litter tables, chairs, or floors with waste papers and other scraps.

7) Projects and other co-curricular requirements like laboratory works should not be done inside the library.

8) Handle books and other library materials with care. Donâ€™t fold the book page/s, avoid writing marks, carving chairs and tables, destroying or tearing books and other materials.

9) Keep things in order. Push your chair back against the table.

10) Courtesy must be observed at all times. Respect yourselves and others.

11) Inquiries concerning lost and found items inside the library should be done at the information desk. The library is not responsible for any losses.


Students who fail to comply with the rules of the library will be denied in the privileges upon recommendation of the librarian as the case may be.


Only the card holders are entitled to enjoy the privileges of borrowing of the library resources and services. It should be presented to the librarian at the counter wherever a student borrows.
