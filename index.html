VISION

The Library Department of Jose Rizal Memorial State University – Tampilisan Campus envisions a free and equal access of library resources and quality services to every member of the ASEAN academic community.

MISSION

We commit ourselves to provide various formats of library materials both print, non-print and electronic media. It further commits continual progress for global competitiveness through Integrated Library System to meet the wide range demand of the 21st century academic community learners.

GOALS

1. Help attain the vision and mission of the university;

2. Serve the clientele in providing more than enough and valuable information;

3. Provide various media to support the curriculum;

4. Compete globally in the pursuit of the ASEAN Integration.
