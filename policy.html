LOAN POLICY

Note: Return the borrowed books PROPERLY!


How to secure library ID?


Library ID is essential in getting inside the library premise. Anyone who cannot present library ID means non admittance to the library and never be cleared during clearance signing.


REQUIREMENT AND PROCEDURE


* ENLIST your name, course and year on the sheet of paper provided at the bulletin board situated in front of the library entrance/exit door;


* WAIT until your name will be posted in the “On Call” and submit 2 pcs. 1x1 id picture with clear background.


LIBRARY REQUIREMENTS


FACULTY (Regular) - JRMSU ID card and Teaching Load

FACULTY (Visiting/Part-time) - Teaching Load, Dean’s approval as guarantor

STUDENTS - Library ID

OUTSIDE USERS - Any Valid ID, Recommendation Letter, P100.00/day
